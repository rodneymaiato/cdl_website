About CDL

Most bookkeeping problems aren’t “accounting” problems. They’re workflow problems: missing receipts, messy vendor names, uncategorized transactions, and a month-end scramble. We fix that with a consistent close process that keeps your books clean and your stress low.

What we believe

  • Consistency beats “hero work” at tax time.
  • Clean inputs create clean reports.
  • Boundaries protect quality.
  • Compliance isn’t optional; it’s the baseline.

What you can expect

  • A repeatable monthly close — same checklist, every month.
  • Receipt control that reduces back-and-forth.
  • Clear communication (no mystery work, no vague updates).
  • Books that are defensible and decision-ready.

How we work

Simple workflow. Tight review. No chaos.

Setup + standards

We lock in categories, rules, and a receipt flow so the books don’t drift month to month.

Monthly review

We reconcile, review exceptions, clean vendor data, and fix issues before they compound.

Close + clarity

You get reports plus a short summary that tells you what matters—without the accounting lecture.

Fit matters

We’re selective because quality and compliance require it.

We’re a fit if…

  • You want clean books every month, not once per year.
  • You’re willing to follow a simple receipt flow.
  • You prefer clear boundaries and predictable pricing.

We’re not a fit if…

  • You want “cheap” more than you want “correct.”
  • You dump receipts once a quarter and hope for the best.
  • You expect unlimited extras inside a flat monthly fee.
